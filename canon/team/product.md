> **Example team file.** Replace the bracketed bits with your reality.

---

## What Product owns

- The roadmap and prioritization.
- Discovery, specs, and product definition.
- The product catalogue (born in your docs system; the [`products/`](../../products/) MDs are pointers).

## Anti-scope — what Product does NOT own

- Delivery dates and implementation (Engineering).
- Commercial pricing of a product (Sales / Leadership).

## Hard rules

1. **A product is born in your docs system, then mirrored as a thin pointer MD** with `docs_url`. Never hand-author product content in the repo expecting it to flow back.
2. **Never commit a roadmap item as "we will" before it's scoped.** Use "we plan to" until Engineering has sized it.

## What Product's signals and recaps must never contain

- Unreleased roadmap shared under customer NDA.
- Competitive roadmap intel attributed to a named source without clearing it.

## Escalation path

- Roadmap conflict across teams → Product Lead + Leadership.
- Feature gap blocking a deal → Product Lead (Sales routes it here).
