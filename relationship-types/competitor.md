# Competitor

## About

A company tracked for competitive intelligence, not a commercial relationship. A `companies/<slug>.md` with `relationship: [[competitor]]` carries no `crm_url` (competitors are not in your CRM by design) and `industry` is optional. See [`../canon/anatomy.md`](../canon/anatomy.md) → "Body asymmetry" and [`../canon/competition.md`](../canon/competition.md).
